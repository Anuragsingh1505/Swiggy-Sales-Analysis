# Swiggy's Sales analysis-key insights-

## 

###### Executive Summary-

###### This dashboard provides a complete, interactive analysis of Swiggy’s food delivery performance across India. It highlights how customers order, which states/cities drive the most revenue, how sales fluctuate monthly, weekly, and daily, and how food preferences and customer feedback shape overall performance.

###### It gives business leaders a clear, data-backed picture of revenue, customer satisfaction, and operational efficiency.



##### Key Insights -

###### &nbsp;Revenue \& Customer Behavior-



1. Swiggy generated ₹53.01M in total sales across the selected period.
   
2. With an Average Order Value of ₹268, customers prefer quick, affordable food purchases.
   
3. The platform received 5.59M+ ratings, indicating massive user engagement.
   
4. Customer satisfaction is strong with an average rating of 4.34.



###### Time Trends



1. January, May, and August show the highest monthly sales peaks.
   
2. Sales dipped during February, signalling a seasonal slowdown.
   
3. Saturday shows the highest daily revenue (₹7.8M), confirming strong weekend demand.
   
4. Monday and Tuesday remain the slowest days, ideal for weekday discounts.
   
5. Weekly sales trend shows consistent demand but slight volatility around weeks 16, 24, and 30.



###### Food Preferences-



1. Non-Veg orders dominate (65%), contributing nearly twice the revenue of Veg dishes.
   
2. Veg orders still maintain significant share in metro and North Indian states.



###### Geographic Insights-



1. Maharashtra and Karnataka lead the country in total revenue.
   
2. North Indian states like Delhi, Uttar Pradesh, and Haryana show strong growth.
   
3. Eastern and North-Eastern states generate lower revenue — a potential expansion area.



###### City-Level Insights-



1. Bengaluru tops city-wise revenue, followed by Mumbai, Lucknow, Hyderabad, and New Delhi.
   
2. Major metros dominate, showing stronger food delivery culture and higher disposable income.



###### Quarterly Performance-



1. Q1 and Q2 outperform Q3 in revenue and number of orders.
   
2. Q3 shows a drop in demand — likely due to monsoon or seasonal slowdown.



###### Recommendations-



* Launch weekend premium offers to leverage peak demand.



* Introduce mid-week discounts to boost Monday–Tuesday sales.



* Expand restaurant partnerships in low-performing states.



* Promote Veg meals in cities with strong vegetarian preference.



* Use rating data to identify restaurants that require quality improvements.
