Swiggy Sales Analysis Dashboard (Excel Project)

Project Overview-

This project presents an interactive Excel Dashboard built using PivotTables, PivotCharts, Map Visualizations, Slicers, and KPI Cards.
It analyzes Swiggy’s food delivery performance across India, helping understand customer behavior, sales trends, food preferences, and regional performance.

KPIs-

⦁	Total Sales (₹)

⦁	Average Rating

⦁	Rating Count

⦁	Total Dishes

⦁	Average Order Value (₹)

Charts Included-

⦁	Monthly Sales Trend (Line chart)

⦁	Daily Sales Trend (Column chart)

⦁	Weekly Sales Trend

⦁	Sales by Food Type (Veg vs Non-Veg)

⦁	Sales by State (Geo Map)

⦁	Quarterly Performance Summary

⦁	Top 5 Cities by Sales

⦁	Restaurant, Category, and Month slicers for dynamic filtering

Key Insights-

✔ Total sales crossed ₹53M
✔ Weekend performance strongest (Sat: ₹7.8M)
✔ Non-Veg contributes 65% of total revenue
✔ Maharashtra & Karnataka lead state-wise sales
✔ Bengaluru is the highest revenue-generating city
✔ Avg rating stays strong at 4.34
✔ Q1 and Q2 outperform Q3

Recommendations-

⦁	Increase restaurant partnerships in Tier 2–3 cities

⦁	Offer Monday–Tuesday promotions

⦁	Improve low-rated restaurant performance

⦁	Strengthen presence in low-revenue states

⦁	Use food preference insights for targeted campaigns
